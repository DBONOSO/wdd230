OUR PURPOSE

Our purpose is to gather all of the businessmen in the area of San Rafael, Mendoza, to motivate the communication and cooperation between them. Our focus is agriculture and tourism and We pursue the creation of new job opportunities in those sectors.



TARGET-AUDIENCE

Who is the target audience?

The target audience of this chamber are agriculture and tourism and businessmen and enthusiastn, and students

Two personas

  1. A 23 year-old male, who is studying agriculture business, is working on his final theshis for his degree. Since He has a busy schedule He needs to obt all the possible information available online.
  2. Old man who wants to investing in the vineyard field and need to find more information about the differentes sectors of this entrepreneurship in the other side He wants to find more contacts and opportunities to give form his proyect

Scenarios

  1. First persona wants to know about the community events to the chamber of commerce to increase their network.
  2. Second persona wants to know how many members are in the chamber of commerce
  3. Third persona wants to see the benefits to be part of the chamber of commerce. How much does He have to pay to be part?
  4. fourth persona wants to find a new job in the company of the chamber of commerce. What are the company that conformed part of the chamber of commerce? In the page I have numbers or email to these companies?
  5. Fifth persona wants to find more places to make a tourist tour in the canyons and lakes. Where are the key places for tourists?
